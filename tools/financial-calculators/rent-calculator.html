📐 Rent Cost Formula Pattern

M=R+U+P+I+O
LT=M×m
M = monthly housing total
R = rent
U,P,I,O = utilities, parking, insurance, other
LT = lease recurring total
m = lease months

Yearly projections apply the annual increase rate to rent for each future year.

❓ Rent Calculator FAQs

What is included in monthly housing cost?+
It includes rent plus utilities, parking, insurance, and any other monthly costs you enter.
Does lease total include deposit and one-time fees?+
Lease total shown as recurring cost is monthly spending × lease months. Move-in cost separately includes deposit and one-time fees.
How is projected future rent calculated?+
Each year's rent is increased by the annual rent increase rate you provide, then monthly total is recalculated.
