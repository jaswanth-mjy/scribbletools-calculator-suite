Formula

Stacked discounts are applied sequentially, not added directly.

Sale Price = P × (1 − d₁/100) × (1 − d₂/100)
Final = Sale Price + Tax + Shipping

This helps you compare true checkout cost when tax and shipping are included.

Quick Tips

- A 20% + 10% stacked discount equals 28% total, not 30%.

- Always compare final checkout amount, not just sale banner percentage.

- Tax is usually charged on discounted amount in most regions.
