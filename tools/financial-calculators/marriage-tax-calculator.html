📘 How the Comparison Is Calculated

This tool compares two scenarios using the same total household income:

  • Scenario A: current spouse-wise income and deductions (default real-life case).
  • Scenario B: total household income split equally between spouses (what-if planning scenario).
Tax Impact = Tax(Equal Split) − Tax(Current Spouse-wise)
Positive = equal split gives higher tax
Negative = equal split gives lower tax

The calculator uses India slab rates, rebate logic (where applicable), and 4% cess for the selected FY. It is for planning only.

💡 Why Differences Happen

  • Uneven spouse income: Slab progression can make split vs non-split distributions differ in total tax.
  • Rebate thresholds: If one scenario pushes taxable income below rebate limits, tax can reduce significantly.
  • Deduction usage: Eligible deductions entered for each spouse can change taxable income asymmetrically.
  • Cess impact: 4% cess applies on tax after rebate, affecting final payable amount.
