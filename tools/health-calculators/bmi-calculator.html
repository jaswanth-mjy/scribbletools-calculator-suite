Understanding BMI

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple calculation using a person's height and weight. The formula is BMI = kg/m² where kg is your weight in kilograms and m² is your height in meters squared.

📊 BMI Categories

Underweight: BMI less than 18.5
Normal weight: BMI 18.5–24.9
Overweight: BMI 25–29.9
Obese: BMI 30 or greater

💡 Why Calculate BMI?

BMI is a useful measure of overweight and obesity. It helps assess your risk for chronic diseases like heart disease, diabetes, and high blood pressure.

⚠️ BMI Limitations

BMI doesn't measure body fat directly. Athletes with high muscle mass may have high BMI but low body fat. Consult healthcare professionals for complete assessment.
