Health Recommendations

Understanding BMI

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple calculation using height and weight. Formula: BMI = kg/m²

📊 BMI Categories

Underweight: <18.5
Normal: 18.5–24.9
Overweight: 25–29.9
Obese: ≥30

💡 Why Calculate?

BMI helps assess risk for chronic diseases like heart disease, diabetes, and high blood pressure.

⚠️ Limitations

BMI doesn't measure body fat directly. Athletes may have high BMI but low body fat.

🎯 Ideal Range

A healthy BMI is typically 18.5-24.9. Individual needs may vary by age and body type.
