Marriage Tax Calculator

About the Marriage Tax Calculator

Instantly estimate the difference in federal income tax if you file jointly as a married couple versus separately as singles. Enter both incomes to see if you get a marriage bonus or penalty. Great for tax planning, budgeting, or understanding how marriage affects your taxes.

Note: The data used in this calculator was last updated on July 30, 2024, and may change in the future.

Use Cases:
  • Compare taxes for married vs. single filing
  • Plan for marriage or major life changes
  • Understand the marriage bonus or penalty
  • Check homework or prepare for finance class
