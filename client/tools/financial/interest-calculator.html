About the Interest Calculator

Instantly calculate the interest earned or paid on any amount, for any rate and time period. Enter your principal, interest rate, and years to see the total interest and final amount. Great for savings, loans, or quick financial planning.

Note: The data used in this calculator was last updated on July 30, 2024, and may change in the future.

Use Cases:
  • Estimate interest earned on savings or investments
  • Calculate interest paid on loans or credit cards
  • Compare different interest rates or time periods
  • Check homework or prepare for finance class
