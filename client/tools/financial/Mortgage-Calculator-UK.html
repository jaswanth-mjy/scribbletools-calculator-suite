UK Mortgage Calculator

Estimate your monthly mortgage payments for properties in the UK. Simple, fast, and accurate.

How to Use

  1. Enter Property and Loan Details:
    • Input the property price and your deposit amount.
    • Provide the annual interest rate and the mortgage term in years.
  2. Choose Mortgage Type:
    • Select either 'Repayment' or 'Interest Only'.
  3. Calculate:
    • Click "Calculate Payments" to see your monthly cost.
