Mortgage Calculator

About the Mortgage Calculator

The Mortgage Calculator helps you estimate your monthly mortgage payments and understand the total cost of homeownership. This essential tool is perfect for home buyers, refinancers, and anyone planning to purchase a property.

How Mortgages Work:
A mortgage is a loan specifically for purchasing real estate. The loan is secured by the property itself, meaning the lender can foreclose if you fail to make payments. Mortgage payments typically include principal, interest, taxes, and insurance (PITI).

How to Use This Calculator:
  1. Home Price: Enter the total purchase price of the property
  2. Down Payment: Enter either the percentage or amount (the other will auto-calculate)
  3. Loan Term: Enter the mortgage term in years (typically 15 or 30 years)
  4. Interest Rate: Enter the annual mortgage interest rate
  5. Advanced Options (Optional): Include property taxes, insurance, HOA fees, and PMI
  6. Click Calculate: View your monthly payment and complete mortgage schedule
  7. Download Excel Schedule: Get the full payment schedule for your records

What You'll Discover:
  • Monthly Payment: Your total monthly mortgage payment (PITI)
  • Payment Breakdown: How much goes to principal, interest, taxes, and insurance
  • Total Payment: Total amount paid over the life of the mortgage
  • Total Interest: Total interest paid over the mortgage term
  • PMI Impact: How private mortgage insurance affects your payments

Perfect For:
  • Home Buyers: Calculating affordability and comparing mortgage options
  • Refinancers: Evaluating if refinancing will save money
  • Real Estate Investors: Analyzing potential investment properties
  • First-Time Buyers: Understanding all components of a mortgage payment
  • Financial Planners: Helping clients budget for homeownership

Pro Tips for Mortgage Planning:
  • Aim for 20% down payment: Avoids PMI and may qualify you for better rates
  • Compare 15 vs 30-year terms: 15-year saves interest but has higher payments
  • Consider total housing costs: Include maintenance, utilities, and repairs
  • Get pre-approved: Know your budget before house hunting
  • Lock in your rate: Protect against rate increases while you shop

Common Mortgage Scenarios:
  • First-Time Home Buyer: 30-year term with 5-10% down payment and PMI
  • Move-Up Buyer: 20% down payment on a larger home, avoiding PMI
  • Refinancing: Lowering interest rate on existing mortgage
  • Investment Property: Higher interest rate and larger down payment required

Note: This calculator provides estimates for planning purposes. Actual mortgage terms may vary based on credit history, lender requirements, location, and other factors. Always consult with mortgage professionals for personalized advice.
