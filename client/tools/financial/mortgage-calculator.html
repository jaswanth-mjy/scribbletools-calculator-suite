About the Mortgage Calculator

The Mortgage Calculator helps you determine your monthly home loan payment and understand the total cost of homeownership. Whether you're a first-time buyer or considering refinancing, this tool provides essential information to make informed decisions about one of life's biggest financial commitments.

How Mortgages Work:
A mortgage is a loan secured by real estate that allows you to purchase a home by paying a portion upfront (down payment) and borrowing the rest. You then repay the loan with interest over a fixed period, typically 15-30 years.

How to Use This Calculator:
  1. Home Price: Enter the total purchase price of the home you're considering
  2. Down Payment: Amount you'll pay upfront (typically 10-20% of home price)
  3. Loan Term: Length of the mortgage in years (15, 20, or 30 years are common)
  4. Interest Rate: Annual percentage rate (APR) offered by your lender
  5. Click Calculate: See your monthly payment and total loan costs

What You'll Learn:
  • Shorter Terms: 15-year loans have higher payments but save significant interest
