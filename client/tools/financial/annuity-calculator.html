About the Annuity Calculator

Instantly see the future value or present value of an annuity based on your payment, interest rate, and number of periods. Great for retirement planning, savings, or understanding how regular investments grow over time.

Note: The data used in this calculator was last updated on July 30, 2024, and may change in the future.

Use Cases:
  • Estimate the value of regular savings or retirement contributions
  • Plan for future income from an annuity or pension
  • Compare different savings or investment plans
  • Check homework or prepare for finance class
