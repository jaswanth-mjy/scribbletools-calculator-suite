💡 Money-Saving Tips for College Textbooks

  • Buy Used: Save 25-50% by purchasing used textbooks in good condition
  • Rent Textbooks: Consider rental options for books you won't keep long-term
  • Digital Editions: E-textbooks are often cheaper and more portable
  • Compare Prices: Check multiple sources including campus bookstore, Amazon, Chegg
  • Sell Back: Recoup costs by selling textbooks at semester end
