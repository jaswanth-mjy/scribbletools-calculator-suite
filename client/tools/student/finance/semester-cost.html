💰 College Budget Planning Tips

  • Create a Budget: Plan all semester expenses before classes start
  • Emergency Fund: Set aside 10-15% for unexpected college costs
  • Track Spending: Monitor actual vs. planned expenses throughout the semester
  • Find Scholarships: Apply for financial aid and scholarships to reduce costs
  • Compare Options: Evaluate housing, meal plans and textbook alternatives
  • Plan Multiple Semesters: Create long-term education budget for degree completion

📊 Common College Expense Categories

• Tuition & Fees: Core academic costs, lab fees, technology fees

• Housing: Dorm rooms, apartments, utilities, insurance

• Meals: Dining plans, groceries, restaurants

• Textbooks: Course materials, digital access codes, supplies

• Transportation: Gas, parking, public transit, flights home

• Personal: Entertainment, clothing, health, emergencies
