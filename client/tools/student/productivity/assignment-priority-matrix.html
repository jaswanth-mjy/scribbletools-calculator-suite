📋 How to Use the Assignment Priority Matrix

Organize your assignments using the proven Eisenhower Matrix method. Categorize tasks by urgency and importance to focus on what matters most and improve your academic productivity.

🎯 Prioritization Benefits

  • Better time management and focus
  • Reduced academic stress and overwhelm
  • Improved assignment completion rates
  • Strategic approach to deadline management

📊 Matrix Categories

  • Do First: Urgent + Important
  • Schedule: Important + Not Urgent
  • Delegate: Urgent + Not Important
  • Eliminate: Neither Urgent nor Important

💡 Student Success Tips

Eisenhower Method: Focus on important tasks first. Urgent doesn't always mean important. Strategic Planning: Regularly review and adjust your matrix to stay on top of changing priorities and new assignments.
