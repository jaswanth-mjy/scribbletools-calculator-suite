Focus & Productivity Tips

Eliminate Distractions

Put your phone in another room or use apps to block distracting websites. Create a dedicated study space free from interruptions.

Stay Hydrated

Dehydration can significantly reduce cognitive performance. Keep water nearby and drink regularly during study sessions.

Fuel Your Brain

Eat brain-boosting foods like nuts, berries, and dark chocolate. Avoid heavy meals that can cause energy crashes.

Move During Breaks

Use breaks to stretch, walk around, or do quick exercises. Physical activity boosts blood flow to the brain.

Follow the 20-20-20 Rule

Every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds to reduce eye strain and maintain focus.

Use Focus Music

Instrumental music, white noise, or nature sounds can enhance concentration. Avoid music with lyrics during complex tasks.
