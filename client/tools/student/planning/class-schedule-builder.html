Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Class Schedule Builder completely free?

Yes! Our class schedule builder is 100% free with no hidden costs, subscriptions, or premium features. You can create unlimited schedules, download as PDF, and use all features without any charges.

Do I need to create an account to use this tool?

No registration required! You can start creating your schedule immediately. Your data is saved locally in your browser, so you can access it anytime without logging in.

Can I add weekend classes (Saturday and Sunday)?

Absolutely! Our schedule builder supports all seven days of the week, including Saturday and Sunday, making it perfect for students with weekend classes or study sessions.

How do I download my schedule as a PDF?

Simply click the "Download as PDF" button after creating your schedule. The tool will generate a professional-looking PDF that you can save, print, or share with others.

What time slots are available?

The schedule supports time slots from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM in one-hour increments. This covers typical academic schedules for most institutions.

Will my schedule be saved if I close the browser?

Yes! Your schedule is automatically saved in your browser's local storage. As long as you use the same browser and don't clear your browsing data, your schedule will be there when you return.

Can I edit or delete classes after adding them?

Yes! You can easily remove any class by clicking the remove (×) button on the class card. To edit, simply delete the old entry and add a new one with updated information.

Is this tool mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! The schedule builder is fully responsive and works perfectly on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. You can create and manage your schedule on any device.

Can I print my schedule directly?

Yes! Use the "Print Schedule" button for a clean, print-optimized version of your schedule. All unnecessary buttons and controls are hidden during printing for a professional look.

What information can I add for each class?

For each class, you can add the class name, instructor/professor name, room number or location, day of the week, and time slot. This helps you have all important information in one place.
