How to Use the Grade Calculator

Calculate Current Grade:
1. Add your assignments, quizzes, tests, and final exam scores
2. Adjust the weight percentage for each category
3. Click "Calculate Current Grade" to see your overall grade

Calculate Grade Needed:
1. Switch to the "Grade Needed" tab
2. Enter your current grade in the class
3. Enter your desired final grade
4. Enter the final exam weight
5. Click calculate to see what score you need on the final

Note: Make sure all category weights add up to 100% for accurate results. The calculator uses weighted averages to determine your final grade.
