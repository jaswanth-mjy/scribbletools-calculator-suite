Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Study Planner completely free?

Yes! Our study planner is 100% free with no hidden costs, subscriptions, or premium features. You can create unlimited schedules, subjects, goals, and tasks, and use all features without any charges.

Do I need to create an account to use this tool?

No registration required! You can start planning your studies immediately. Your data is saved locally in your browser, so you can access it anytime without logging in or creating an account.

What features are included in the Study Planner?

The planner includes five main features: (1) Weekly Schedule with 7-day planning and time slots from 8 AM to 9 PM, (2) Subject Management to track courses with instructor info and study hours, (3) Goal Tracking for daily, weekly, and monthly goals, (4) Task Manager with priority levels and due dates, and (5) Statistics Dashboard showing your progress and completion rates.

How does the goal tracking system work?

You can set three types of goals: daily (for today's tasks), weekly (for the current week), and monthly (for long-term objectives). Each goal has a description, target date, and completion status. The system tracks your progress and shows completion rates in the statistics section.

Will my data be saved if I close the browser?

Yes! All your schedules, subjects, goals, and tasks are automatically saved in your browser's local storage. As long as you use the same browser and don't clear your browsing data, everything will be there when you return.

Can I export my study plan data?

Yes! You can export all your data (schedules, subjects, goals, and tasks) as a JSON file. This allows you to back up your data or transfer it to another browser. Simply click the "Export Data" button in the planner.

How do task priorities work?

Tasks can be assigned three priority levels: High (urgent and important), Medium (important but not urgent), or Low (can be done later). High priority tasks are displayed with a red indicator, medium with orange, and low with blue, helping you focus on what's most important.

Is the Study Planner mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! The study planner is fully responsive and works perfectly on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. You can access and update your schedule, goals, and tasks from any device, anywhere.

Can I link tasks to specific subjects?

Yes! When creating a task, you can link it to a specific subject from your subject list. This helps you organize tasks by course and see at a glance which assignments belong to which class.

What statistics does the planner track?

The statistics dashboard shows: total number of subjects, total scheduled study blocks, total goals (with completion rate), and total tasks (with completion rate). This gives you a comprehensive overview of your academic planning and progress.
