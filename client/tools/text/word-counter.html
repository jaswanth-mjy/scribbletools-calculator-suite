Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as a word?

Words are separated by spaces, tabs, or line breaks. Hyphenated words count as one word, and contractions like "don't" count as one word.

How is reading time calculated?

Reading time is estimated based on an average reading speed of 200-250 words per minute for adults.

Is my text stored anywhere?

No, all text analysis happens in your browser. Your content is never sent to our servers or stored anywhere.
