Binary / Hex / Octal Converter

Convert numbers between binary, hexadecimal, octal, and decimal formats. Essential for programmers, students, and anyone working with different number systems.

How to use:

  1. Paste or type your number in any base (e.g. 1010, 0xA, 12, 014).
