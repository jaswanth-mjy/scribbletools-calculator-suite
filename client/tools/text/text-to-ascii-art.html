Text to ASCII Art

Convert your text into ASCII art banners using simple block letters. Great for fun, banners, or making your messages stand out in plain text!

  • ASCII Art: Instantly turn your text into blocky ASCII art banners.
  • Fun & Unique: Use for social media, code comments, or to grab attention in plain text.
  • Great for: Messaging, posts, bios, creative writing, and code headers.

How to use:

  1. Paste or type your text into the input box below.
  2. Click Generate ASCII Art to convert your text.
  3. View the ASCII art result in the output area.
  4. Use Copy to copy the result, Download to save as a file, or Clear to reset.

Example uses: Make your social media posts stand out, create fun banners, or add ASCII art to your code comments!
