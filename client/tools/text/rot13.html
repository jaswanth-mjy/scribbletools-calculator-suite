ROT13 Encoder / Decoder

The ROT13 Encoder/Decoder tool lets you quickly encode or decode text using the classic ROT13 cipher. ROT13 replaces each letter with the letter 13 positions ahead in the alphabet, making it a simple way to obfuscate spoilers, puzzle answers, or private notes. Since ROT13 is symmetric, encoding and decoding are the same operation.

  • Encode/Decode: Instantly apply ROT13 to your text. The same button both encodes and decodes.
  • Simple Obfuscation: Hide spoilers, puzzle solutions, or sensitive info in forums, emails, or games.

How to use:

  1. Paste or type your text into the input box below.
  2. Click ROT13 Encode/Decode to transform your text.
  3. View the encoded or decoded result in the output area.
  4. Use Copy to copy the result, Download to save as a file, or Clear to reset.
