About the Fat Intake Estimator

Instantly estimate your daily fat intake in grams based on your calorie goal and fat percentage. Enter your calories and fat percent to see your daily fat target. Great for meal planning, fitness, or tracking your nutrition.

Note: The data used in this calculator was last updated on July 30, 2024, and may change in the future.

Use Cases:
  • Plan your daily fat intake for health or weight loss
  • Compare fat needs for different calorie goals
  • Track your nutrition for health or performance
  • Check homework or prepare for health class
