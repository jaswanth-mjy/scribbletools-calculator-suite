About the Healthy Weight Range Checker

Instantly see a healthy weight range for your height and gender. Enter your details to see a recommended weight range for good health. Great for setting goals, tracking progress, or learning about healthy living.

Note: The data used in this calculator was last updated on July 30, 2024, and may change in the future.

Use Cases:
  • Set healthy weight goals
  • Compare healthy ranges for different heights or genders
  • Monitor health and wellness progress
  • Check homework or prepare for health class
