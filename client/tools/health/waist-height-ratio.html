About the Waist-to-Height Analyzer

Instantly estimate your waist-to-height ratio, a simple indicator of health and risk. Enter your waist and height to see your ratio and risk category. Great for health, fitness, or tracking your wellness journey.

Note: The data used in this calculator was last updated on July 30, 2024, and may change in the future.

Use Cases:
  • Check your risk for heart disease or metabolic issues
  • Track your waist-to-height ratio for health and fitness goals
  • Compare ratios for different ages or genders
  • Check homework or prepare for health class
