Due Date Finder

About the Due Date Finder

Instantly estimate your baby's due date based on the first day of your last period. Enter your date to see your estimated due date. Great for expectant parents, planning, or learning about pregnancy.

Note: The data used in this calculator was last updated on July 30, 2024, and may change in the future.

Use Cases:
  • Estimate your due date for pregnancy planning
  • Track pregnancy milestones
  • Check homework or prepare for health class
