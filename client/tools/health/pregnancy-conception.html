About the Conception Date Estimator

Instantly estimate your conception date based on your due date. Enter your estimated due date to see when conception likely occurred. Great for expectant parents, planning, or learning about pregnancy.

Note: The data used in this calculator was last updated on July 30, 2024, and may change in the future.

Use Cases:
  • Estimate conception date for pregnancy planning
  • Track pregnancy milestones
  • Check homework or prepare for health class
