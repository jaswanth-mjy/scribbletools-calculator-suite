About the Running Pace Calculator

Calculate your running pace per kilometer or mile with precise time tracking. Enter your distance and time to get detailed pace analysis for training and racing.

Pace Formats Calculated:
  • Per Kilometer: Minutes and seconds per km
  • Per Mile: Minutes and seconds per mile
  • Speed: Kilometers or miles per hour
  • Total Time: Complete duration breakdown

Use Cases:
  • Plan race strategies and pacing
  • Track training progress over time
  • Compare performance across distances
  • Set goal times for upcoming races
