About the Medical BSA Calculator

Instantly estimate your body surface area (BSA) for medical dosing or health assessments. Enter your weight and height to see your BSA. Great for healthcare, fitness, or medical planning.

Note: The data used in this calculator was last updated on July 30, 2024, and may change in the future.

Use Cases:
  • Estimate BSA for medication dosing
  • Check BSA for health or fitness assessments
  • Compare BSA for different weights or heights
  • Check homework or prepare for health class
