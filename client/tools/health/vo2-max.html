VO2 Max Estimator

About the VO2 Max Estimator

Calculate your VO2 Max using multiple validated testing protocols. VO2 Max measures your body's maximum oxygen consumption and is the gold standard for aerobic fitness.

Test Types:
  • Cooper 12-min: Distance covered in 12 minutes
  • Rockport Walking: 1-mile walk with heart rate
  • Custom Distance: Any distance run at maximum effort

VO2 Max Categories (ml/kg/min):
  • Excellent: >50 (men), >45 (women)
  • Good: 43-50 (men), 36-45 (women)
  • Average: 35-42 (men), 27-35 (women)
  • Below Average: <35 (men), <27 (women)
