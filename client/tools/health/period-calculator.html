About the Menstrual Cycle Tracker

Instantly track your menstrual cycle and predict your next period. Enter your last period and cycle length to see your next period date. Great for planning, health, or learning about cycles.

Note: The data used in this calculator was last updated on July 30, 2024, and may change in the future.

Use Cases:
  • Track your menstrual cycle and next period
  • Plan for travel, events, or health appointments
  • Check homework or prepare for health class
