About the Carb Intake Estimator

Instantly estimate your daily carbohydrate intake in grams based on your calorie goal and carb percentage. Enter your calories and carb percent to see your daily carb target. Great for meal planning, fitness, or tracking your nutrition.

Note: The data used in this calculator was last updated on July 30, 2024, and may change in the future.

Use Cases:
  • Plan your daily carb intake for health or weight loss
  • Compare carb needs for different calorie goals
  • Track your nutrition for health or performance
  • Check homework or prepare for health class
