About the BMI Calculator

Our free BMI (Body Mass Index) calculator helps you quickly determine your BMI and health category based on your height and weight. BMI is a widely used screening tool that indicates whether your weight is in a healthy range for your height.

Simply enter your weight in kilograms and height in centimeters to get instant results with your BMI score and corresponding health category.

How to Calculate BMI

Step-by-Step Instructions:

  1. Enter Your Weight: Input your current weight in kilograms (kg)
  2. Enter Your Height: Input your height in centimeters (cm)
  3. Click Calculate: Press the calculate button to get your BMI
  4. View Results: Your BMI score and health category will be displayed instantly

Formula: BMI = Weight (kg) ÷ Height² (m²)

Frequently Asked Questions

What is BMI?

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a measure that uses height and weight to estimate if an adult is a healthy weight. It's calculated by dividing weight in kilograms by height in meters squared.

Is BMI accurate for everyone?

BMI is a useful screening tool but has limitations. It doesn't account for muscle mass, bone density, or body composition. Athletes or very muscular individuals may have high BMI but low body fat.

What should I do if my BMI is outside the normal range?

If your BMI indicates you're underweight, overweight, or obese, consult with a healthcare professional for personalized advice on achieving or maintaining a healthy weight.

Can I use this calculator for children?

This BMI calculator is designed for adults. Children and teenagers have different BMI ranges that account for age and sex. Consult a pediatrician for children's BMI assessment.
