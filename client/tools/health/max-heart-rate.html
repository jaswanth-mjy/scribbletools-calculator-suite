Max Heart Rate Tool

About the Max Heart Rate Tool

Calculate your maximum heart rate using multiple scientifically validated formulas. Different formulas may be more accurate for different populations and activity levels.

Formulas Used:
  • Classic: 220 - age (most common)
  • Tanaka: 208 - (0.7 × age) (revised formula)
  • Gulati (Women): 206 - (0.88 × age)
  • Nes: 211 - (0.64 × age) (active individuals)

Use Cases:
  • Establish baseline for target heart rate zones
  • Set safe exercise intensity limits
  • Monitor cardiovascular fitness progress
  • Plan cardio training programs
