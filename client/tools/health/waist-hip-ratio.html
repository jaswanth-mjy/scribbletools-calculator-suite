About the Waist-to-Hip Ratio Tool

Instantly estimate your waist-to-hip ratio, a key indicator of health and body fat distribution. Enter your waist and hip measurements to see your ratio and risk category. Great for health, fitness, or tracking your wellness journey.

Note: The data used in this calculator was last updated on July 30, 2024, and may change in the future.

Use Cases:
  • Check your risk for heart disease or metabolic issues
  • Track your waist-to-hip ratio for health and fitness goals
  • Compare ratios for different ages or genders
  • Check homework or prepare for health class
