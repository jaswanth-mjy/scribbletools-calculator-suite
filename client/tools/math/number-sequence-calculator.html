About Number Sequences

Arithmetic Sequence: A sequence where each term is found by adding a constant (common difference) to the previous term.
Geometric Sequence: A sequence where each term is found by multiplying the previous term by a constant (common ratio).

Formulas:
  • Arithmetic: aₙ = a₁ + (n-1)d
  • Geometric: aₙ = a₁ × rⁿ⁻¹
  • Sum of Arithmetic: Sₙ = n/2 × (a₁ + aₙ)
  • Sum of Geometric: Sₙ = a₁ × (1 - rⁿ) / (1 - r) (for r ≠ 1)
Use Cases:
  • Identifying patterns in data
  • Solving math and science problems
  • Financial calculations (interest, payments, etc.)
