About the Rectangle Calculator

Calculate area and perimeter of a rectangle from length and width dimensions.

Formulas:
  • Area: A = length × width
  • Perimeter: P = 2(length + width)
  • Diagonal: d = √(length² + width²)
Use Cases:
  • Room and floor area calculations
  • Construction and architectural planning
  • Material estimation for rectangular surfaces
  • Geometry homework and educational purposes
