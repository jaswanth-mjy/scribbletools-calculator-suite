About the Cylinder Calculator

Calculate all properties of a cylinder from radius and height: volume, surface areas, and circumference.

Formulas:
  • Volume: V = πr²h
  • Total Surface Area: A = 2πr² + 2πrh = 2πr(r + h)
  • Lateral Surface Area: A_lateral = 2πrh
  • Base Area: A_base = πr²
  • Circumference: C = 2πr
Use Cases:
  • Engineering and manufacturing calculations
  • Container and tank capacity planning
  • Material estimation for cylindrical objects
  • Educational geometry and physics problems
Why use this calculator? Quick and accurate calculations for all cylinder properties with clear formulas.
