About the Regular Polygon Calculator

Calculate area, perimeter, angles, and other properties of regular polygons from the side length.

Formulas:
  • Area: A = (n × s²) / (4 × tan(π/n))
  • Perimeter: P = n × s
  • Interior Angle: θ = (n-2) × 180° / n
  • Exterior Angle: φ = 360° / n
  • Apothem: a = s / (2 × tan(π/n))
  • Circumradius: R = s / (2 × sin(π/n))
