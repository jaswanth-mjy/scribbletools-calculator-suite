About the Triangle Calculator

Calculate area, perimeter, and other properties for different types of triangles.

Formulas:
  • Right Triangle: A = ½ × base × height
  • General Triangle (Heron's): A = √[s(s-a)(s-b)(s-c)]
  • Equilateral: A = (√3/4) × side²
  • Perimeter: P = a + b + c
