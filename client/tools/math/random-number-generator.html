About the Random Number Generator

What is this?
A versatile random number generator for creating random numbers with customizable ranges and options. Perfect for gaming, statistics, sampling, decision making, and educational purposes.

Features:
  • Customizable number ranges (integers and decimals)
  • Generate single or multiple numbers
  • Unique number generation option
  • Sorting and formatting options
  • Quick presets for common use cases
  • Statistical analysis of generated numbers
  • Number sequence generation
  • Generation history tracking
  • Copy and download functionality
