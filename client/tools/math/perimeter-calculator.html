About the Perimeter Calculator

Quickly find the perimeter or circumference of common shapes like rectangles, circles, triangles, and more. Just select your shape, enter the measurements, and see the result. Perfect for measuring borders, fencing, or any project where you need to know the total distance around a shape.

Use Cases:
  • Calculate the length of fencing needed for a yard or garden
  • Find the border length for craft or sewing projects
  • Measure the distance around a running track or pool
  • Check homework or prepare for geometry class
