About the Volume Calculator

Instantly find the volume of common 3D shapes like cubes, spheres, cylinders, cones, and more. Just select your shape, enter the measurements, and see the result. Great for science, construction, packing, or any project where you need to know how much space something takes up.

Use Cases:
  • Calculate how much liquid a container can hold
  • Estimate the amount of soil, sand, or gravel needed for a project
  • Find the volume of boxes for shipping or storage
  • Check homework or prepare for geometry class
