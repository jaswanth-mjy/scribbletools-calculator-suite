How to Use

  1. Upload Your Image:
    • Drag & drop your image into the upload area
    • Or click "Choose File" to browse
  2. Select Grid:
    • Choose a grid layout (e.g., 3×3 for 9 posts)
    • Use square images for best results
  3. Generate & Download:
    • Click "Generate Grid"
    • Download all pieces and post in order

Common Uses & Tips

📦 Common Uses

  • Create 3×3 hero grids for profile highlights
  • Showcase portfolio items across multiple posts
  • Build teaser mosaics for campaigns

💡 Pro Tips

  • Use square images or crop to square beforehand
  • Post left→right, top→bottom to preserve order
  • Keep consistent captions/hashtags across pieces
