About the Rotate Image Tool

Rotate images by 90°, 180°, 270°, or any custom angle. Supports clockwise and counterclockwise rotation.

Features:
  • Drag & drop interface for easy file selection
  • Quick preset angles (90°, 180°, 270°) and any custom angle
  • Clockwise/counterclockwise direction
  • Lossless rotation for JPEG/PNG/WebP
  • Custom output format selection
  • Before/after preview comparison
  • Instant download of rotated images
  • Batch rotate multiple images (open multiple tabs)
  • Works offline, no upload required
Use Cases:
  • Fix sideways or upside-down photos from phones/cameras
  • Rotate scanned documents for correct orientation
  • Prepare images for design, printing, or presentations
  • Rotate artwork, diagrams, or screenshots
  • Creative effects: rotate for collages, memes, or social media
  • Correct orientation for profile pictures, ID photos, etc.
  • Rotate images for web publishing or e-commerce
Privacy: All processing happens in your browser. Your images never leave your device. Files are automatically deleted after 10 minutes.
